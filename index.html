Add team member

Step 1 of 4
Navigate to Team
In the sidebar, click Team under Management to see all your team members.
Step 2 of 4
Click "Add team member"
Use this button to open the form. You can add stylists, barbers, therapists, and other team roles.
Step 3 of 4
Fill in the details
Enter the team member's name, email, phone, and assign their role and permissions.
Step 4 of 4
Save & send invite
Once all fields are complete, the button turns purple. Click to add them and send an invite email.
